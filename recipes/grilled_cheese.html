Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese is on of the most simple dishes that a person can make when in the kitchen. Even though this dish is very simple does not mean it is lacking in flavor.

Ingredients

  • Bread of choice
  • 1/4 cup of mozzrella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of american cheese
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. 1. Place cheese between 2 slices of bread
  2. 2. Heat pan so butter can be melted, but do not let butter brown
  3. 3. Toast one side of bread in buttered pan on medium until desired level of browness
  4. 4. melt remaining butter in pan and flip sandwhich so other side of bread can read desired level of browness and cheese can melt.
  5. 5. Remove from heat cut diagonally to have triangle pieces. eat and enjoy.
